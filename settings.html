About JELVIN POS

JELVIN POS is a free, open-source point of sale system built specifically for Ghanaian small businesses and market sellers. It works 100% offline and stores all data securely in your browser.

This system was designed with Ghanaian market realities in mind, featuring MoMo integration, Cedi currency support, and offline functionality for areas with unreliable internet connectivity.
